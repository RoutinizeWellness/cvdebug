## üö´ What NOT to Do (DR Killers)

### Avoid These Tactics:
1. **Link Farms** - Immediate Google penalty
2. **PBN (Private Blog Networks)** - Risky, can tank DR
3. **Paid Link Schemes** - Against Google guidelines
4. **Excessive Reciprocal Links** - Looks manipulative
5. **Low-Quality Directories** - Spam signal
6. **Comment Spam** - Wastes time, no value
7. **Forum Signature Spam** - Ignored by Google
8. **Automated Link Building** - Easy to detect
9. **Irrelevant Links** - Hurts topical authority
10. **Too Fast Link Velocity** - Unnatural pattern
